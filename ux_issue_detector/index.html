UX Issue Detector

Check every symptom you observe in your interface. The detector maps friction points to root causes and gives you a prioritized fix list.

How It Works

Each symptom is linked to an impact level and a root cause. Check every issue you've observed — the detector aggregates them into a prioritized report.

  • High impact — directly breaks user flows or blocks conversions
  • Medium impact — creates friction and increases drop-off
  • Low impact — minor annoyances that compound over time
