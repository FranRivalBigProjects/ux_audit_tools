Industry Benchmarks

These thresholds are based on published research and industry standards.

  • SUS ≥ 68 = above average usability. SUS ≥ 80 = excellent (Grade A).
  • NPS ≥ 0 = more promoters than detractors. NPS ≥ 50 = excellent loyalty.
  • Task Success Rate ≥ 78% = industry average for well-designed interfaces.
  • Time on Task: users spending more than 2× the target time signals a critical flow problem.
