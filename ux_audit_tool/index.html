How the Audit Works

Each dimension is scored 0–100 and multiplied by its weight to produce a composite UX audit score. Issues are flagged with priority levels based on impact and score.

  • Navigation carries the highest weight (25%) — it's the foundation of usability
  • Scores below 40 trigger a High priority flag — immediate action needed
  • Scores 40–65 trigger a Medium priority flag — improvements recommended
  • Scores above 65 are considered healthy — minor optimizations only
